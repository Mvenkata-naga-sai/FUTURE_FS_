# Agni Maati — Website Pitch

## The Business

**Agni Maati** is a cozy upscale bistro in Austin, Texas, built around a wood-fired kitchen, locally sourced ingredients, and an intimate dining room that feels like a beautifully renovated farmhouse.

---

## The Problem

Most local restaurant websites are generic, slow, and forgettable — a stock photo hero, a PDF menu, and a Google Maps embed. Diners decide whether to book a table in the first 3 seconds. If the website doesn't feel like the restaurant, they move on.

---

## What We Built

A cinematic, scroll-driven single-page website that puts the food and atmosphere front and center — the way a great restaurant should be experienced before you even walk in the door.

### Sections
- **Hero** — Bold headline, reservation CTA, atmospheric background
- **Story** — The restaurant's character and philosophy
- **Menu** — Curated preview of Starters, Mains, and Desserts
- **Atmosphere** — Visual gallery capturing the dining room feel
- **Visit** — Hours, address, and contact
- **Reservations** — Inline reservation request form

### Technical Stack
- React + Vite (fast, modern)
- Framer Motion animations (cinematic scroll reveals)
- Tailwind CSS (responsive, mobile-friendly)
- AI-generated food photography (zero stock photo budget)
- Fully deployable — goes live with one click

---

## How It Helps the Business Grow

| Before | After |
|---|---|
| Generic template site | Distinctive brand identity online |
| No reservation flow | Inline reservation request form |
| Static, lifeless design | Cinematic scroll experience |
| Poor mobile experience | Fully responsive on all devices |
| No SEO structure | Semantic HTML, proper headings |

### Specific Growth Drivers
1. **More reservations** — The "Reserve a Table" CTA appears twice above the fold and again in the dedicated section. Friction is zero.
2. **Word-of-mouth shareable** — A site this distinctive gets shared. Diners send the link to friends before visiting.
3. **First impressions** — 70% of diners research a restaurant online before visiting. This site converts curious visitors into booked guests.
4. **Brand consistency** — The warmth and craft of the physical space is now reflected digitally. Trust is established before they arrive.
